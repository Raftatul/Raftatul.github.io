The Atrium - Unreal Engine 5 Blueprints

The Atrium is a team school project, I work on the Camera, Controller

I have contribute to the design of the 3C and coding it.

  • Camera

    The camera was designed to feel alive and dynamic without giving the sensation of motion sickness.

    Here is a list of elements implement:

    • Camera tilt, a little based on the x velocity relative to the camera of the player.
    • Camera Bobbing: Apply camera bobbing when the player is moving on the ground using Camera Shake.
      The shake force depend of the run state of the player.
    • The FOV increase when the player fall to enhance the sense of speed.

  • Controller

    I worked on the bump ability and the movements of the controller.

    Bump Ability:

    • Project the player in the opposite direction he is facing when bumping on a surface.
