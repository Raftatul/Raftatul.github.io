Square Roots ! - PROGRAMMER/DESIGNER

This project was created during the Global Game Jam was developed using Unity and inspired by the theme "Root".

In the game, players grow a tree by creating branches and roots. The branches collect sunlight and clouds, while the roots collect nutrients and water underground.

Proper management of resources such as water, sunlight, air, and nutrients is essential to grow the tree.

On this project, I work as a programmer / designer, my duties included:

  • Programming the roots and branches animations using DoTweens.
  • Designing the game loop.
