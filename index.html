About Me

Hi, I am a Technical Designer student at Isart Digital Montreal. I have a great interest in making game development easier by making tools and I love prototyping features from other games. I mainly use C# languages and GDScripts (Godot programming language)

My ultimate goal is to become a technical game designer, where I can use my skills and knowledge to help bring game ideas to life through programming and technical implementation. I am always excited to continue learning about programming and game engine features.
